About JOLKHABAR

Welcome to JOLKHABAR, your trusted source for authentic Bengali culinary delights and traditional puja items.

Our Story

JOLKHABAR started with a passion for preserving and sharing the rich culinary heritage of Bengal. We specialize in traditional Bengali food items, from various achars (pickles) to mouth-watering delicacies, and provide complete puja kits for all your spiritual needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the authentic taste of Bengal to your doorstep while maintaining the highest quality standards. We take pride in our traditional recipes and processes, ensuring that each product delivers the genuine Bengali flavor you love.
